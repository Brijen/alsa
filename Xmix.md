The `xmix` mixer
----------------

`xmix` is a [mixer program](/Mixer "Mixer") which works with ALSA's
[OSSEmulation](/OSSEmulation "OSSEmulation"). See
[http://freshmeat.net/projects/xmix/](http://freshmeat.net/projects/xmix/)
for further information.
