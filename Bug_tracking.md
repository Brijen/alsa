Bug tracking
============

### From the ALSA wiki

Jump to: [navigation](#mw-head), [search](#p-search)

The Mantis bugtracking system has been introduced and is used for ALSA:

[http://bugtrack.alsa-project.org/alsa-bug/](http://bugtrack.alsa-project.org/alsa-bug/)

When using the bug tracker, please include the output of the
[aadebug](/Aadebug "Aadebug") script.

Retrieved from
"[http://alsa.opensrc.org/Bug\_tracking](http://alsa.opensrc.org/Bug_tracking)"
